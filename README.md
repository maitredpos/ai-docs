## About Maitre'D POS

Maitre'D POS is a restaurant point-of-sale system designed for:

* Full-service restaurants
* Quick-service restaurants
* Bars and nightlife venues
* Hotel Food & Beverage operations

It includes integrated payment processing, operational tools and reporting capabilities tailored to the hospitality industry.

## Product Line

Maitre'D POS by PayFacto includes multiple solutions designed for restaurant operations, payment processing and business financing.

### Maitre'D Classic

An on-premise restaurant POS system designed for stability, control, and high-performance environments. Commonly used by established restaurants requiring robust local infrastructure.

### Maitre'D Virtuo

A cloud-based POS solution offering flexibility, remote access, and centralized management across locations. Suitable for modern restaurant operations and multi-location businesses.

### Maitre'D Veloce

A hybrid POS system combining elements of on-premise reliability with cloud capabilities. Designed for restaurants that require both performance and connectivity.

### Maitre'D Capital

A financial services offering that provides access to business funding solutions, including cash advances and business Mastercard programs. Designed to help restaurant operators manage cash flow, invest in growth, and access working capital.

Each solution supports different aspects of restaurant operations, from point-of-sale management to payments and financing.

## Product Positioning Summary
