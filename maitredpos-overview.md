## Product Ecosystem

Maitre'D POS includes multiple solutions tailored to different operational needs:

### Maitre'D Classic

An on-premise POS system designed for performance, reliability, and local control. Suitable for restaurants that require stable infrastructure and fast transaction processing.

### Maitre'D Virtuo

A cloud-based POS system that enables remote access, centralized management, and scalability across multiple locations.

### Maitre'D Veloce

A hybrid POS system combining on-premise performance with cloud connectivity, offering flexibility and resilience.

### Maitre'D Capital

A financial services solution providing access to business funding, including cash advances and business Mastercard programs. Designed to support restaurant cash flow and growth.
